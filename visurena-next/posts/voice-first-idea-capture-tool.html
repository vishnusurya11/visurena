When working on a project, getting your requirements across should be easy. For most of us, it's often quicker and clearer to say what we want out loud, instead of writing it all down. That's the motivation behind the tool I built.

How It Works

This tool lets you speak your requirements directly. It records your voice, transcribes what you say, and gives you a written version of your idea. From there, you can quickly refine or hand it off to an AI (like ChatGPT or a cloud service) to make the text more professional or actionable.

Here's the flow:

  1. Click 'Live Recording' to start.
  2. Choose your recording input, select a model, and hit record.
  3. When you're done, the tool generates a transcript of your spoken requirement.
  4. You can take that text and use AI to polish or rephrase it for better clarity.

More Than Just Live Recording

There's also a file upload feature. If you already have audio or video files, you can upload them:

  • The tool automatically separates the audio from video (if needed).
  • It transcribes the audio into text.
  • You can process multiple files at once and save all transcripts to a chosen folder.
